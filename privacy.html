Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Together is designed with your privacy in mind. Your personal reflections and progress stay on your device and your own iCloud account. We do not sell your data.

1. Who we are

Together: Men's Mental Health is developed and operated by Kristian Clifford. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at together@cliffordcoaching.no.

2. What data is stored on your device

Together stores the following data locally on your device using your browser's localStorage:

  • Completed exercises and streaks
  • Mood check-in history
  • Journal entries
  • App preferences and settings
  • Notification schedule preferences

This data is also synced to your own iCloud account using Apple's iCloud Key-Value storage, so it is preserved if you delete or reinstall the app, or move to a new iPhone signed into the same Apple ID. This iCloud data is stored under your Apple ID and is not accessible to us. You can clear this data at any time by deleting the app and turning off iCloud sync for Together in your device's iCloud settings.

3. What data is shared with third parties

Apple (App Store & In-App Purchases)
When you download the app or purchase a Pro subscription, Apple processes payment information according to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive your payment details.

RevenueCat
We use RevenueCat to manage Pro subscriptions. RevenueCat receives an anonymous device identifier to verify your subscription status. No personal information (name, email, or location) is shared. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Formspree (Bug reports)
If you choose to submit a bug report through the app, the report is sent via Formspree. Bug reports are anonymous and contain only the message you write. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.

4. Local notifications

Together can send you optional daily reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers in connection with notifications.

5. Analytics and tracking

Together does not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking. We do not track your location, contacts, or browsing activity.

6. Children's privacy

Together is rated 18+ and is not intended for use by children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

7. Data retention and deletion

All data stored on your device is retained until you delete the app. To delete your subscription data held by RevenueCat, contact us at together@cliffordcoaching.no and we will submit a deletion request on your behalf.

8. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold. Since Together stores no personal data on our servers, your primary right is to delete the app and its local data from your device.

For questions about your rights or data held by RevenueCat, contact us at together@cliffordcoaching.no.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Together after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
